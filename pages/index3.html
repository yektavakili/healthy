Tomato & Onion Filled Kabab Tamatar (Tomatoes) 4 large Pyaz (Onions) 4 large Beef qeema (Mince) 500g Pyaz (Onion) chopped ½ Cup Cut thick slices of tomatoes,remove its center part from each slice,chop finely & reserve for later use (approx. 1 Cup) & set aside tomato rings.
Kabab Tocos with Salsa -Tamatar (Tomatoes) 4 medium -Pyaz (Onion) peeled 1 medium -Hari mirch (Green chillies) 3-4 -Lehsan (Garlic) cloves 3-4 On a heated griddle,place tomatoes,onion,green chillies,garlic & roast from both sides until charred (6-8 minutes).
Spicy Chicken Kabab -Lal mirch powder (Red chilli powder) 1 & ½ tbs or to taste -Namak (Salt) 1 tsp or to taste -Dhania powder (Coriander powder) 1 & ½ tbs -Baisan (Gram flour) roasted ½ tbs In a bowl add red chilli powder, salt, coriander powder, gram flour, red chilli crushed, cumin powder, mace powder, nutmeg powder, citric acid, close the container & shake well.Spice mix is ready!
